Early Years

My name is Iva Milanova, 37 years, married, mother of a little princess. I live in Sofia with my family. I have graduated from primary school in 1995 - "7-Th School", Sofia. Then I graduated from 18 "William W. Gladstone", Sofia - with french profile. After that I was interested in different directions of life and science. I was excited about the Law, traveling, photography and more. Then I came into the circles of the translators of goodwill. That led me to Subs.sab.bz, where I found a lot of new friends. We were translating movies to the popular site Zamunda and enjoyed every praise.One "Thanks" was the greatest joy. My work on translating movies is very similar to coding now. Subtitle programs work with tags and scripts, as well as frames and seconds, milliseconds, limited space, and many parameters.A movie of normal length is amateurish translated for about 45 days. This means that the translator works slowly and methodically. As our main job in the last 18 years, our family has our own business, in which we basically throw all our resources.

Over time, various hobbies appeared in my life. I like to knit, I love to shoot with the camera, I like to do quilting, paint and make minor repairs with color corrections at home. My hobbies can be find in the links bellow - Etsy and Pinterest

Now I'm going to another area - creating sites. I have passed throw the SoftUni courses for: HTML, CSS, JS, Jquery Bootstrap, etc. I am steel learning this huge material but I will not stop. I wish one day to become Senior Fron-end Developer, so I will work hard in this direction.

Fifoza

Fifoza- this nickname uppears in my life more than 15 years ago. It keeps inself the tiny name Fifi. I have many accounts with this nick name and steel love it. These days have been good experience for me to learn how to comunicate with big mass of people - unpasionate to get the newest subtitles for some long waiting movie.

After that I`ve stop is translating and have take the most important role in my life - to be mother. All this time the nick Fifoza was steel alive. That's why I decided to use it again when I started to learn HTML, CSS, JS, Web Design and C# courses in SoftUni, Sofia.
